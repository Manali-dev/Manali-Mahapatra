Information To Know About frontend

 

The frontend is an abstraction, simplifying the underlying component by providing a user-friendly interface, while the back usually handles data storage and business logic. In telecommunication, the front can be considered a device or service, while the back is the infrastructure that supports provision of service.
A rule of thumb is that the client-side (or "frontend") is any component manipulated by the user. The server-side (or "backend") code usually resides on the server, often far removed physically from the user.
